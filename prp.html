During a PRP session blood is drawn from a patient's arm. The blood is then spun to extract the plasma. Plasma is a substance found in the blood that contains platelets. PRP after having a hair transplant will improve follicle viability, enhancing post-transplant tissue healing and promotes hair growth in transplanted hair.
